About Us

Based in Calamba, Laguna, an art brand specializing in handmade, custom wood mosaic wall art and decor, often featuring intricate geometric patterns and abstracts.

Created by a local artist, these unique pieces utilize various wood scraps and stains to create textured, aesthetic, and functional home decor.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the wood mosaic made of?▼
We use high-quality pinewood and different kind of hardwood (such as madre de cacao, mahogany, yakal, narra, traviesa, and acacia), and the surface is termite treated with environmentally friendly coatings, preserving the natural wood grain.
Is it easy to install?▼
It's very simple. Each product comes with an expansion bolt, screws and installation takes only a few minutes.
Do you support customization?▼
Yes, we do customization depending on your desired size requirements. If you require specific sizes, please contact our customer service team directly for details.
How to maintain wood finishes?▼
It is strongly suggested to avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight or overly humid environments. When cleaning, simply wipe away dust gently with a feather duster or a dry, soft cloth.
Is there a difference between the actual product and the pictures?▼
There is a minimal difference due to the natural characteristics of wood, the grain and color of each piece of wood are slightly different, which is precisely what makes each wooden decoration unique and charming.
How long does it take for the order to be shipped after it's placed?▼
On-hand stock products are usually shipped within the day or 2 days; custom-made products require 5-7 business days for production.
How much is the shipping cost?▼
Shipping costs depends based on the preferred courier (Lalamove, LBC or J&T).
For NCR/Laguna area, Lalamove is recommended.
For Luzon area, we strongly recommend Victory Liner Drop & Go Courier service which will take 2-3 days upon order drop off.
For Visayas and Mindanao area, LBC and J&T are our preferred courier service.
What payment methods are supported?▼
Payment methods are through bank transfer or GCash.
What should I do if I receive a damaged item?▼
Please take photos and contact us within 48 hours of receiving the package. If the item is damaged during shipping, we will resend it to you free of charge or issue a refund.
